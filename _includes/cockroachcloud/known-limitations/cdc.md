- Changefeeds only work on tables with a single [column family](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/column-families.html) (which is the default for new tables).
- Changefeeds do not share internal buffers, so each running changefeed will increase total memory usage. To watch multiple tables, we recommend creating a changefeed with a comma-separated list of tables.
- Many DDL queries (including [`TRUNCATE`](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/truncate.html) and [`DROP TABLE`](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/drop-table.html)) will cause errors on a changefeed watching the affected tables. You will need to [start a new changefeed](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/create-changefeed.html#start-a-new-changefeed-where-another-ended).
- Changefeeds cannot be [backed up](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/backup.html) or [restored](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/restore.html).
- Partial or intermittent sink unavailability may impact changefeed stability; however, [ordering guarantees](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/stream-data-out-of-cockroachdb-using-changefeeds.html#ordering-guarantees) will still hold for as long as a changefeed [remains active](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/stream-data-out-of-cockroachdb-using-changefeeds.html#monitor-a-changefeed).
- Changefeeds cannot be altered. To alter, cancel the changefeed and [create a new one with updated settings from where it left off](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/create-changefeed.html#start-a-new-changefeed-where-another-ended).
- Additional target options will be added, including partitions and ranges of primary key rows.
- Changefeeds do not pick up data ingested with the [`IMPORT INTO`](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/import-into.html) statement.
- Using a [cloud storage sink](../{{site.versions["cloud"]}}/create-changefeed.html#cloud-storage-sink) only works with `JSON` and emits [newline-delimited json](http://ndjson.org) files.
