- [Statement hints]({% link {{ page.version.version }}/cost-based-optimizer.md %}#statement-hints) do not apply to statements within views. The workaround for `REWRITE INLINE HINTS` is to modify the inline hints directly in the body by replacing the view. There is no workaround for `SET VARIABLE` hints.
- [Statement hints]({% link {{ page.version.version }}/cost-based-optimizer.md %}#statement-hints) do not apply to statements within routines. The workaround for `REWRITE INLINE HINTS` is to modify the inline hints directly in the body by replacing the routine. There is no workaround for `SET VARIABLE` hints.
