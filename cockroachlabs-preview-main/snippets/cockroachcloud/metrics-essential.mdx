title: "Essential Metrics for CockroachDB Standard Deployments"
old_description: "Learn about the recommended essential metrics for monitoring your CockroachDB Standard cluster."
---

These essential CockroachDB metrics let you monitor your CockroachDB Standard cluster. The metrics are available on graphs on the tabs listed in the **Metrics tabs** column. Where **Custom** is listed, the metric may be graphed in a <a href="/docs/VERSION_PLACEHOLDER/cockroachcloud/custom-metrics-chart-page" data-versioned="true">**Custom Metrics Chart**</a>. The **Usage** column explains why each metric is important to visualize and how to make both practical and actionable use of the metric in a production deployment.
