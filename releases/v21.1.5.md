### Bug fixes

- Fixed a panic that could occur in the optimizer when executing a prepared plan with placeholders. This could happen when one of the tables used by the query had computed columns or a partial index. [#66883][#66883]

[#66883]: https://github.com/cockroachdb/cockroach/pull/66833
